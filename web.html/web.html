Executive Summary

About Sonny Horizon

Sonny Horizon Travel Agency (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic and customer-centric travel agency established to provide comprehensive travel solutions for individuals, corporate clients, and government institutions. The company specializes in local and international travel bookings, including flights, accommodation, and fully managed business travel services.

Strategically positioned in Mbombela, Mpumalanga — an area known for tourism and business activity — the agency efficiently serves both domestic and international travellers.

By focusing on both peak and off-peak seasons, Sonny Horizon ensures consistent revenue flow and market relevance throughout the year.
