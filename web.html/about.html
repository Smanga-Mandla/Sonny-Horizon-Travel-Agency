About Us

Sonny Horizon Travel Agency

We are a dynamic, customer-centric travel agency based in Mbombela, Mpumalanga — strategically positioned close to some of South Africa's most iconic tourism destinations including Kruger National Park and the Panorama Route.

We specialise in local and international travel bookings — flights, accommodation, and fully managed travel services for individuals, corporate clients, and government institutions.

Whether you travel for business or leisure, in peak season or off-peak, Sonny Horizon ensures you experience travel that creates memories that never fade.
